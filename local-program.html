Our Local Recovery Pathway

Hope for recovery is a 2 year community based, faith to freedom, recovery pathway. It offers individuals an opportunity to explore faith as a recovery model.

The program is split into 6 x 12 week groups, with each course offering a unique faith aspect for change. At the end of each group, participants have a chance to gain a pass or a pass with merit certificate depending upon their attendance and their score in the end of course quiz. At the end of the 2 years, a final graduation certificate will be presented.

Hope for Recovery is an excellent way for individuals to explore faith in a relaxed, friendly and fun atmosphere. It's also a great course for individuals with a faith to strengthen their beliefs in order to move forward into a more positive lifestyle.

Program Structure

📅 Year 1 Courses

  • Reveal, Release & Recovery
  • Restoring Broken Relationships
  • Holistic Health

📅 Year 2 Courses

  • Fruit of Freedom
  • Overcoming Temptation
  • 3 S's for Sobriety

🎓 Certification

Each 12-week course offers pass or pass with merit certification. After completing the 2-year program, participants receive a graduation certificate.
