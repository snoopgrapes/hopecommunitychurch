Stories of Transformation

Meet individuals who have found hope, healing, and wholeness through our recovery programs

Mark's Journey

18 months in recovery | Former addiction

"I came to Hope for Recovery broken and hopeless. Today I'm living free and helping others."

Mark struggled with addiction for over a decade, losing his job, home, and family connections. After hitting rock bottom, he found Hope Community Church's recovery program.

Through the 12-week "Fruit of Freedom" course and ongoing community support, Mark rebuilt his life from the ground up.

Key Milestones:

  • Completed both 12-week recovery courses
  • Reconnected with his family after 3 years
  • Now volunteers as a recovery group facilitator
  • Celebrated 18 months of sobriety

Sarah's Journey

9 months in recovery | Anxiety & depression

"The Holistic Health course taught me how to care for my whole self - body, mind and spirit."

Sarah battled severe anxiety and depression for years, which led to isolation and hopelessness. Medication alone wasn't providing the breakthrough she needed.

Through the church's Holistic Health program, Sarah learned practical tools for managing her mental health while building a supportive community.

Key Milestones:

  • Completed Holistic Health 12-week course
  • Reduced anxiety medication by 50%
  • Started volunteering at Community Cafe
  • Leading a small support group for women

David's Journey

2 years in recovery | Alcohol dependency

"The Faith to Freedom pathway gave me structure when my life had completely fallen apart."

David's alcohol dependency cost him his career and nearly his marriage. After multiple failed attempts at recovery, he committed to the full 2-year Faith to Freedom pathway.

Through consistent support, mentorship, and spiritual growth, David has not only maintained sobriety but is now training to become a recovery coach.

Key Milestones:

  • Completed the full 2-year recovery pathway
  • Restored relationship with his wife and children
  • Now enrolled in recovery coach training
  • Celebrating 2 years of continuous sobriety

Real Hope, Real Transformation
