At Hope Community Church Clydebank, we're committed to addressing life's challenges through faith, practical support, and genuine community connection.

Our Mission & Vision

Hope Community Church exists to be a place of hope, healing, and wholeness for everyone in Clydebank and beyond. We believe in addressing the whole person - spiritual, emotional, physical, and social - through a comprehensive network of support services and faith-based programs.

Hope for Recovery: A Dynamic Movement

Our recovery ministry is a dynamic movement where lives are radically transformed through the power of the Gospel. We equip individuals with battle-tested tools through our 6 × 12-week courses — a scriptural arsenal for lasting victory over addiction and life-controlling issues.

Faith to Freedom Recovery Pathway

A structured 2-year journey to lasting transformation through our comprehensive recovery programs:

  1. Initial connection through Community Cafe or Sunday services
  2. 12-week courses (Fruit of Freedom & Holistic Health)
  3. Community integration through church life and small groups
  4. Leadership development opportunities

Each 12-week course can be taken individually or as part of the complete pathway

Certificate upon completion Flexible participation

Recovery Course Details

Holistic Health Course: Focuses on physical, emotional, mental and spiritual health components, helping participants understand how these areas interconnect in recovery.

Fruit of Freedom Course: Based on the nine Fruit of the Spirit (Love, Joy, Peace, Patience, Kindness, Goodness, Faithfulness, Gentleness, Self-Control) to transform negative behaviors and attitudes.

Certification: Participants can earn a Pass certificate (attend 6 sessions + 50% quiz score) or Merit certificate (attend 9 sessions + 75% quiz score).
