Your journey to freedom starts here

Hope Recovery is a unique, community-driven recovery program that blends faith with freedom, designed to help individuals overcome addiction and lead a more positive lifestyle. Rooted deeply in faith, the program creates a welcoming, relaxed environment where participants can explore spiritual growth alongside practical recovery strategies. Whether participants are new to faith or seeking to strengthen existing beliefs, Hope Recovery provides a pathway toward healing and transformation.

How do we help?

Structured Recovery Courses

A progressive journey of healing.
Our program is built on six distinct, faith-based 12-week courses. Each course focuses on a different aspect of faith and recovery, providing a clear pathway from initial hope to lasting freedom. Participants receive certificates for each course completed, building a sense of achievement, with a final graduation certificate marking their full journey.

Interactive Peer Support

You are not alone.
We foster deep connections through peer-to-peer small group discussions, case studies, and personal reflection. This isn't just listening; it's sharing and learning together in a safe, welcoming environment. This interactive approach allows you to gain insights from others who understand, bridging the gap between theory and real-life application.

Prevention & Education

Stopping addiction before it starts.
We are expanding our mission into schools with pilot programs that educate young people on the dangers of addiction. Through powerful "This Is My Story" sessions and wellbeing workshops, we foster awareness and resilience in the next generation.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm nervous about coming to a group for the first time. What should I expect? +

It's completely normal to feel nervous! When you arrive, you'll be warmly welcomed in a non-judgmental environment. Our groups begin with establishing group guidelines to ensure everyone feels safe and respected. You can participate as much or as little as you're comfortable with - there's no pressure to share until you're ready.

Do I need to be Christian to attend? +

No, our programs are open to everyone regardless of religious background or beliefs. While our approach is biblically-based, we welcome people from all faiths or no faith at all. Our primary goal is to provide support and practical tools for recovery in a compassionate environment.

What if I relapse during the program? +

Recovery is a journey with ups and downs. If you experience a setback, we encourage you to still come to the group. We offer grace and support, not judgment. Many of our facilitators have walked similar paths and understand the challenges of recovery. We'll help you learn from the experience and continue moving forward.
