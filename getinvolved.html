Get Involved

Your time, prayers, and resources can transform lives. Discover how you can be part of bringing hope to our community.

Ways to Make a Difference

Every contribution, no matter how small, helps us continue our mission of bringing hope, healing, and wholeness to those in need.

🙏

Pray

Prayer is the foundational support for our mission. Your prayers make a powerful difference in the lives we serve.

  • Pray for our leaders and volunteers
  • Pray for participants' healing and perseverance
  • Pray for our community outreach programs
  • Subscribe to our weekly prayer updates
💷

Give

Your financial and material gifts sustain our work and allow us to offer services free of charge to those in need.

  • Financial Donations: One-off or recurring gifts
  • In-Kind Donations: Foodbank items welcome
  • Legacy Giving: Consider including us in your will
  • Monthly Support: Become a regular supporter

Volunteer

Offer your time and skills to be a direct part of someone's journey to freedom and transformation.

  • Direct Service: Recovery groups or community café
  • Practical Help: Cleaning, gardening, maintenance
  • Skilled Support: Administration, IT, marketing
  • Event Support: Help with community events
