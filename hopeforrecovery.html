You Matter

Your journey to freedom starts here

Hope Recovery is a unique, community-driven program that blends faith with freedom, designed to help you overcome addiction and lead a more positive lifestyle. Whether you have no faith or are exploring, we provide a welcoming path toward healing.

What We Do

Structured Recovery Courses

A progressive journey of healing. Our program is built on six distinct, faith-based 12-week courses. Each course focuses on a different aspect, providing a clear pathway from initial hope to lasting freedom. Participants receive certificates for each course completed.

⏰ When: Mondays, 11am - 1:30pm

🎓 Certificates upon completion
🗣️ Peer-to-peer discussions
📊 Case studies & reflection
🤝 Supportive community
Read more about the 2-year pathway ▼

Connection: Welcome through our Community Cafe.

Growth: Six 12-week courses.

Integration: Join church life and small groups.

Empowerment: Volunteer, training, or employment.

Prevention & Education

Stopping addiction before it starts. We are expanding into schools with pilot programs that educate young people on the dangers of addiction. Through "This Is My Story" sessions and wellbeing workshops, we foster awareness and resilience.

"Inspirational speakers! The kids have never looked so interested in any topic!" — Teacher, Vale of Leven Academy

See our school program →

Interactive Peer Support

You are not alone. We foster deep connections through peer-to-peer small group discussions, case studies, and personal reflection. This interactive approach allows you to gain insights from others who understand.
